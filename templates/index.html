Comfortable and Affordable Hostels Rooms

We, GoodStay Hostel, situated at indiranagar,bangalore,offer a pocket friendly stay to each patron and welcome them with complete warmth and hospitality.We offer an array of all essential services that are rendered by the hostel for a hassle free stay at no extra costs.
